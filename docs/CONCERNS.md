## Portfolio Judgment

AC14 is valuable, but not as a success demo. It is valuable because it shows the
kind of engineering maturity reviewers rarely see: an unfavorable benchmark
series was preserved, diagnosed, and used to justify a cleaner successor.

The best public framing is: "AC14 generated disciplined negative evidence that
made AC15 sharper."
